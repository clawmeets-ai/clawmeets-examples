# Retail Team

A five-agent digital workforce for retail, restaurant, and service-business owners. In the time a single consultant returns a draft, this team hands back a **site memo, P&L forecast, ops plan, launch campaign, and hiring pack — in parallel**.

## The Team

| Agent | What they do | Deliverables |
|-------|--------------|--------------|
| `@analyst` | Market sizing, site/trade-area evaluation, competitor and customer intelligence | Site memo, competitor map, demand forecast, go/no-go call |
| `@finance` | 24-month P&L, break-even, capex, pricing, scenario analysis | Financial model, break-even chart, capex budget, funding plan |
| `@ops` | Workflow, supply chain, inventory, staffing model, opening checklist | SOPs, vendor shortlist, labor model, opening-day checklist |
| `@marketing` | Positioning, launch campaign, local/digital channels, loyalty | Positioning brief, 90-day launch plan, channel budget, loyalty design |
| `@hr` | Job descriptions, hiring pipeline, onboarding, handbook basics | Job posts, interview rubric, training plan, employee handbook draft |

## Compelling Project Requests

Paste any of these into your coordinator to see the team move. Replace the bracketed details with your own.

### Open a new location

> I'm opening a **second [coffee shop] in [East Austin]** in the next 6 months. Budget is **$[350K] all-in**. I have two candidate sites: **[1450 E 6th — 1,400 sqft, $6.5K/mo NNN]** and **[2100 Manor Rd — 1,100 sqft, $5.2K/mo NNN]**. Give me a full go/no-go package: trade-area and competitor analysis for both sites, 24-month P&L for the recommended site, opening-day ops plan, a 90-day launch campaign, and a hiring plan with job posts ready to publish.

### Launch a new product line

> I run a **[boutique fitness studio]** with **[800 active members]**. I want to launch a **[private-label supplement / apparel / recovery service] line** in the next **8 weeks**. Size the opportunity against my member base, set pricing and margin targets, design the fulfillment/ops model, build a launch campaign that uses my existing member list, and write the part-time role I'll need to hire to run it.

### Convert a slow daypart into a new concept

> My **[Italian restaurant]** is **packed Thursday–Sunday** but does **< $800/night Monday–Wednesday**. I want to turn **Tuesday nights into a [natural-wine bar concept]** using the same space and staff. Validate demand in my neighborhood, build the Tuesday-only P&L (does it clear incremental cost?), design the menu and service model, plan the launch, and tell me if I need to hire anyone new.

### Go from one location to three

> My **[neighborhood bakery]** did **$[1.1M] last year** at one location with **[22%] store-level EBITDA**. I want to open **two more locations in the same metro over 18 months**. Build the expansion playbook: what has to be true at store #2 before I commit to store #3, the capital plan, the operating-partner vs. GM staffing model, and the marketing strategy that doesn't cannibalize store #1.

### Launch a DTC / online extension of a brick-and-mortar

> My **[home goods shop]** has **[4K Instagram followers]** and **~[120 walk-ins/day]**. I want to launch a **DTC online store** in **[90 days]** that ships nationwide. Pick the 20 SKUs to lead with, model the unit economics (blended margin after shipping and returns), choose the platform and fulfillment setup, build the launch campaign to my existing audience, and tell me who I need to hire or contract.

### Respond to a new competitor

> A **[national chain competitor]** just signed a lease **[two blocks from my pet supply store]**. They'll open in **~[4 months]**. Tell me what they're likely to do, where I can't compete and where I *can*, the pricing/assortment/service changes to make before they open, the retention campaign to lock in my best customers, and the financial impact if I do nothing.

### Franchise or license the concept

> My **[fast-casual salad concept]** has **[2 profitable locations]** doing **~$[900K AUV]**. I want to **license or franchise to 3 operators in the next 18 months**. Size the market for franchisees in **[the Southeast]**, model franchisee unit economics and my royalty stream, design the ops playbook I'd hand them, plan the outreach to find the first three, and tell me what corporate headcount I need to support them.

## What to Expect

Each agent works with its own proprietary knowledge base on your machine, in parallel. A typical request above produces **5–10 deliverables in ~15 minutes** — strategic-level work that would normally take weeks of meetings with consultants, brokers, and agencies. You stay in the loop via chat and approve or redirect at any point.
